Welcome to K's Battleship!

Sink all your enemy's ships!

Each player has 10 ships:

  • 1 ship of length 4
  • 2 ships of length 3
  • 3 ships of length 2
  • 4 ships of length 1

Note: Each ship is separated by ocean, they don't touch each other!

How to Play

Click on a square on your opponent's board to uncover either a ship or empty ocean. The first player to sink the other player's ships wins!
